Shipping infos

  • PLACING AN ORDER
    After you gear up by placing an order, you will receive an e-mail to confirm that we have received the order. At the time of shipment, an additional follow up email will be sent. The confirmation will contain the order number, order detail, and shipping method.
  • PAYMENT METHODS
    We accept American Express, MasterCard, Visa, PayPal and Discover for internet orders. All online orders paid by credit card are processed on a secure server.
  • ORDER INQUIRY
    You may check the status of your order on our website at any time. After login click on the "user button" at top right of the page, click the item "My orders" and check your order status.
  • CANCELLATION POLICY
    Once an order is entered into our system, your order cannot be changed. Please contact us at companyName@mail.com for more information on canceling your order. We cannot guarantee that your order can be cancelled once it is entered into our system.
  • Orders are packed and shipped Monday-Friday only.
  • Most orders are shipped within 30 days from the order date.
  • Orders placed on the weekend or on holidays are processed on the next business day.
  • If we are unable to process your order due to inaccurate or incomplete payment information, your order processing may be delayed an additional 2 business days.
  • With a minimum purchase of $50 or more shipping is no cost to you.
